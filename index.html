Join PALTMed as a New Fellow

A simple guide for International Medical Graduates and U.S.-trained fellows to become members of the Post-Acute and Long-Term Care Medical Association (PALTMed / AMDA).

Join PALTMed Now

Why PALTMed Membership Matters

  • Professional credibility in post-acute and long-term care (PALTC)
  • Access to JAMDA, a leading peer-reviewed journal
  • Discounts for national and regional conferences
  • Networking with medical directors and PALTC leaders
  • Eligibility for committees, abstracts, and leadership roles
