Why this Portal?

The transition to U.S. clinical research can be daunting for IMGs. This portal is designed to provide high-yield, actionable steps to ensure your clinical expertise is recognized at national conferences like AMDA/PALTC 2026. By focusing on Rapid Systematic Reviews and Quality Improvement, you can contribute to the field regardless of your current facility's research budget.
